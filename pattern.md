###Pattern

* #### Pattern 產生

	verb + \< subj, obj, prep_obj, be_adj \>
		

* #### Pattern 結構
	
	1. `Semantic roles`：主詞、受詞
	2. `人稱`：John --> SUBJ_3
	3. `時態`：had --> have

	
* #### Pattern 分數

	1. 長 pattern --> 高分

		<pre>sent: I really wish I <u>have</u> someone ...
	    SV: <b>i have</b>
	   SVO: <b>i have someone</b>
	  Args: <b>wish i have someone</b> </pre>


	2. pf, df or pf-idf
